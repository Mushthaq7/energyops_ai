SOLAR PANEL MAINTENANCE GUIDE
Model: SP-2024-X

1. Visual Inspection
- Check for cracks, chips, or discoloration on the panel surface weekly.
- Ensure the frame is securely fastened and free of corrosion.
- Verify that all wiring is intact and not exposed to the elements.

2. Cleaning Procedures
- Clean panels early in the morning or late in the evening to prevent thermal shock.
- Use deionized water and a soft brush. Do not use abrasive chemicals.
- Remove bird droppings, leaves, and dust immediately to avoid hot spots.

3. Inverter Maintenance
- Check inverter display for error codes daily.
- Ensure cooling fans are operational and vents are clear of debris.
- Tighten electrical connections annually during scheduled downtime.

4. Troubleshooting Low Output
- If output drops unexpectedly, check for shading from new vegetation or structures.
- Inspect bypass diodes for failure.
- Verify grid voltage stability.

5. Safety Precautions
- Always de-energize the system before touching electrical terminals.
- Use insulated tools and wear appropriate PPE (gloves, safety glasses).
- Do not work on the roof during high winds or rain.
