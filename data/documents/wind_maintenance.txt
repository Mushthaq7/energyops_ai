WIND TURBINE MAINTENANCE MANUAL
Model: WT-5000-Gen2

1. Gearbox Lubrication
- Check oil levels monthly. Low oil can cause catastrophic gear failure.
- Collect oil samples quarterly for analysis of metal particles.
- Change oil filters every 6 months or if pressure drop exceeds 2 bar.

2. Blade Inspection
- Inspect blades for leading-edge erosion annually using drone photography with high resolution cameras.
- Repair minor surface cracks with approved epoxy filler.
- Check lightning protection receptors for continuity.

3. Yaw System
- Grease yaw bearing and gears every 3 months.
- Test yaw brake holding torque annually.
- Monitor yaw motor current for signs of binding.

4. Generator Alignment
- Check generator-to-gearbox alignment utilizing laser alignment tools every 6 months.
- Misalignment causes vibration and premature bearing failure.
- Tighten mounting bolts to specified torque (see Table 4.2).

5. Emergency Stop System
- Test emergency stop buttons and safety loops monthly.
- Verify hydraulic pitch system accumulator pressure.
